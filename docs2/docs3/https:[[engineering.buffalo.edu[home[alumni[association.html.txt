UB Engineering and Applied Sciences Alumni Association
The UB Engineering and Applied Sciences Alumni Association (UBEAA) helps you stay connected with your school, your fellow alumni, and current students. Together, we can continue the UB School of Engineering and Applied Sciences tradition of excellence.
Mission
The EAA is a volunteer-led organization established to: cultivate a mutually beneficial relationship between the University at Buffalo (UB) School of Engineering and Applied Sciences (SEAS) and its global alumni community; foster pride, loyalty, and a life-long connection to UB SEAS among alumni, students, parents, and friends; and support SEAS students, faculty, and UB. 
Vision
The vision for the UB Engineering and Applied Sciences Alumni Association is to represent the alumni from SEAS and to expand on creating recognition of the school, its achievements, and the achievements of its alumni around the world. The UBEAA strives to foster pride, loyalty, and life-long connections to UB SEAS while leveraging the strengths of alumni.
