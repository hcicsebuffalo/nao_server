AAP 585SEM - Legal Issues in Arts Mgmt
Introduces students to arts and entertainment-related legal issues and trains them to recognize where legal problems might arise.  This course will provide an overview of Contracts, Arts Advocacy, Corporate Structure and Rules, Employment and Labor, Defamation, and First Amendment Law.
Credits: 3
