Nutrition MS
This School of Public Health and Health Professions degree program will prepare you for advanced professional programs, clinical research or careers in industries such as food, hospitality, health facilities, government or private consulting. Students learn research skills in a dynamic, Research 1 environment, as well as functions of nutrients and other dietary constituents and food, diets, and supplements. The program focuses on nutrition and disease, as well as nutrition intervention and policies.
