Engineering Science MS: Data Science Focus
The Engineering Science MS with a course focus in Data Science offers students a comprehensive education in big data and analysis. Students gain knowledge, expertise, and practical training in various areas, including data collection and management, data analytics, scalable data-driven discovery, and fundamental concepts. The curriculum combines solid theoretical foundations with hands-on training, ensuring graduates are well-prepared to address the analytics needs of employers. Additionally, students can explore diverse application areas, further expanding their understanding of real-world data challenges.
