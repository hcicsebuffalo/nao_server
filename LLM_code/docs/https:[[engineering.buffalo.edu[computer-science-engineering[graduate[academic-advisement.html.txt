Graduate Academic Coordinators
The CSE graduate academic coordinators will answer most of your CSE graduate academic advisement questions, help you register for courses, and file the necessary paperwork to help you graduate on time.
Questions?
We've got answers! Send email to the CSE graduate academic coordinators' shared inbox:
csegradcoord@buffalo.edu

Erin and Ann will both receive your message.  Erin typically advises CSE PhD students, and Ann typically advises CSE MS students.
