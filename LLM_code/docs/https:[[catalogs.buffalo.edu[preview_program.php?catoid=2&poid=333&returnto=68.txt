Affordable Housing Advanced Certificate
The Graduate Affordable Housing Advanced Certificate is designed for individuals with an earned bachelor’s degree and interested in gaining further knowledge about issues facing affordable housing in the United States. In the U. S., the pervasiveness of affordability problems among lower-income renters is primarily due to the shortage of appropriately priced housing and the low and decreasing incomes of renters.
