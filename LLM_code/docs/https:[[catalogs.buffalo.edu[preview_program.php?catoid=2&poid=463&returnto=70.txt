Environmental and Water Resources Engineering MS
Now more than ever, the world needs safe drinking water, clean air, and healthy lakes and aquatic ecosystems. The Environmental and Water Resources Engineering MS program prepares graduates to provide just that. Graduates play a vital role in addressing these environmental challenges through careers in research, government, consulting, or the private sector.
