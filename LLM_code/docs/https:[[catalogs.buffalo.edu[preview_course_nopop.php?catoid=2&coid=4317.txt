AAP 512TUT - Project Guidance
This seminar will guide students through the completion of their Master’s project, providing a structured environment and student and faculty feedback. Students will present and discuss their work in progress in class meetings and will have individual meetings with their instructor/advisor.
Credits: 1 - 6
