Bridge Engineering Advanced Certificate
Bridge engineers need the skills and knowledge required to design the next generation of bridges that are safe, durable, environmentally sustainable, and resilient against natural hazards using new materials, methods, and components. Our flexible Advanced Certificate in Bridge Engineering introduces early career participants to bridge engineering and prepares experienced participants to take on leadership roles in design and management of transportation infrastructure elements including bridges and tunnels.
