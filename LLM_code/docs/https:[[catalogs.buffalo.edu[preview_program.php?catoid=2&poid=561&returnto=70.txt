Mindful Counseling for Wellness and Engagement Advanced Certificate
This program meets your need for continuing education in the area of mindfulness counseling for wellness and engagement, if you are in the helping profession and are a professional psychologist, social worker, mental health counselor, school psychologist, school counselor, administrator, lawyer, teacher, occupational therapist, physical therapist, speech-language pathologist, audiologist or doctor. Upon successful completion of this program, you will earn an Advanced Certificate in Mindful Counseling for Wellness and Engagement, registered with the Division of Professional Education, New York State Education Department.
