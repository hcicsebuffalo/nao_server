Nurse Anesthetist DNP
Our nurse anesthetist doctor of nursing practice program prepares students for the challenges they’ll face as Certified Registered Nurse Anesthetists (CRNA). Our nurse anesthetist students receive an education that equips them to assume clinical, educational, research, and leadership positions within this advanced nursing specialty. The program builds on the clinical preparation of nurse anesthetists enhancing their current role and providing learning opportunities that focus on delivering expert care. Program courses, taught exclusively by nurse specialists and CRNAs, are immediately applicable to nursing practice.
