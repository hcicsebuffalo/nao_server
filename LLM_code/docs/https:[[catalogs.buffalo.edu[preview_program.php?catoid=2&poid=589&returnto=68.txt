Pharmacology MS
Our Master’s Degree in Pharmacology is designed to provide an understanding of basic pharmacology, toxicology, drug discovery and translational science, along with an individualized program of original research. Our students are well prepared to continue in doctoral programs in pharmacology or other biomedical sciences, and strong competitors for academic and industry laboratory research positions. As a PhD candidate, you will do research in our highly collaborative environment, present your findings in a variety of settings and acquire the skills of a well-rounded, independent research scientist.
