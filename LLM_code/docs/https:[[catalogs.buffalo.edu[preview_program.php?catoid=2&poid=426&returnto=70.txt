Cybersecurity Advanced Certificate
The Advanced Certificate in Cybersecurity aims to provide students with a holistic comprehension of the diverse aspects of computer security, catering to the demands of our technology-driven society. 
This program encompasses coursework from various departments at UB, such as computer science and engineering, management, mathematics and law. It offers a comprehensive overview of the interdisciplinary nature of computer security while also delivering specialized training tailored to students’ chosen areas of focus.
