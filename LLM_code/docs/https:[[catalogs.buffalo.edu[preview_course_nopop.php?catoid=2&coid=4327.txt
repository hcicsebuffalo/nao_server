AAP 586LEC - Law & IP: Arts Mgmt
This course will supplement the basic, business law issues discussed in AAP 585 with information arts managers and organizations need as they reach out to the public. Topical areas include Privacy Law, Publicity Rights, Copyright, Licensing, and Trademark Law.
Credits: 3
