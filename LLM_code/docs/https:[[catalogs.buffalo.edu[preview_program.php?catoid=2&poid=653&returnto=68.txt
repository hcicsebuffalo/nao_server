Social Work (Advanced Standing) MSW
Unique to our highly ranked MSW program, is an integrated trauma-informed and human rights perspective, offering a transformational lens to focus your social work policy and practice. The advanced standing track is for qualifying applicants with a bachelor of social work (BSW) degree, earned within six years of starting the MSW.

Applicants must have a 3.0 cumulative GPA in their BSW.
