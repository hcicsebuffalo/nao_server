Applied Statistical Analysis Advanced Certificate
This certificate recognizes the importance of quantitative methodology for conducting scientific research and for guiding evidence-based practice in any field, but in particular the field of education. This advanced certificate provides a foundation from which you may pursue either doctoral studies or a career as a researcher whose job entails analyzing statistical data. The certificate is open to any MA or PhD student from any discipline, or students who hold a BA or BS and meet the general requirements for acceptance.
