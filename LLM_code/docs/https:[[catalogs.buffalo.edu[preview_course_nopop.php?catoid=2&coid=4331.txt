AAP 509SEM - Research in Arts Mgmt
This seminar will guide you through the steps of developing a research question, engaging sources, creating a syllabus of readings and how to assess their functionality for your project, setting up field research, formulating an abstract, creating an outline, and what to consider when you start writing. This course prepares students to enter the writing stage of their final project/thesis.
Credits: 3
