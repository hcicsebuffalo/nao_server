Engineering Pedagogies and Practices Advanced Certificate
The Advanced Certificate in Engineering Pedagogies and Practices focuses on the practical application of engineering education research findings in the classroom. The program is designed for engineers who want to know how to effectively communicate and impart engineering knowledge to others in a variety of professional contexts, not just in formal classrooms. 
Required Core (9 credits)
DEE 501SEM - Foundations of Engineering Education Credits: 3
DEE 502SEM - Equity and Inclusion in Engineering Education Credits: 3
DEE 503SEM - Practicum and Mentored Application of Engineering Education Credits: 1  (portfolio to be completed within course)
DEE 599TUT - Supervised Teaching for Engineering Education Credits: 1 - 6 or Other department 599 supervised teaching
