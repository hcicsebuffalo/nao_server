Engineering Science MS: Quantum Science and Nanotechnology Focus
The Engineering Science MS with a course focus on Quantum Science and Nanotechnology is an interdisciplinary program designed to provide students with cutting-edge training in the emerging area of quantum technologies in response to technological advances. This program is unique in providing advanced training in the related underlying physics while combining this knowledge to acquire new cutting-edge innovative engineering and information sciences skills.
