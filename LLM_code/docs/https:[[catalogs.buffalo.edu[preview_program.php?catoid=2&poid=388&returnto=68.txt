Chemistry MS
Within a thriving educational and research environment, the Department of Chemistry makes pioneering discoveries, and advances real-world applications of chemistry. We take pride in our collaborative spirit and strong tradition of graduate mentorship, guiding students toward high-level careers in academia. At UB, you will work with faculty members at the cutting edge of research to impact science, technology and society. The excellence of our graduate training is manifested in job placement in leading industrial and pharmaceutical companies, in government positions, and in faculty and postdoctoral positions at prestigious universities and colleges.
