Medical Anatomy and Experimental Pathology MS
The MS Program in Medical Anatomy and Experimental Pathology provides training in human gross and microscopic anatomy and in the biology of disease and injury. The program is aimed at preparing students for careers in the medical professions, medical/premedical education or biomedical research. Research opportunities with our faculty range from studies of cellular and molecular mechanisms of disease and tissue repair to the development of tools for visualizing biological structure and investigation of instructional careers or engagement in associated industry.  A more research-focused training is offered in our Computational Cell Biology, Anatomy and Pathology MS program.
