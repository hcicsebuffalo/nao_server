Electrical Engineering MS
The Electrical Engineering MS program at UB consists of a foundational academic core supplemented by a comprehensive selection of advanced courses, culminating in a thesis or an equivalent culminating experience. Students have the opportunity to specialize in various areas, including:
Signals, communication and networking.
Optics and photonics.
Solid state electronics.
Energy systems.
