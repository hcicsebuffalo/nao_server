Humanities Interdisciplinary MA
Students in the Humanities MA program, design individual programs, combining coursework in arts and humanities fields such as: African and African American studies, American studies, Asian studies, classics, comparative literature, disability studies, English, history, media study, music, theatre and dance, and visual studies. Students may also choose to integrate one of the above disciplines with a social-or natural-science. There are also several formal concentrations within the humanities interdisciplinary degree, including film and media study, social media and disability studies.
