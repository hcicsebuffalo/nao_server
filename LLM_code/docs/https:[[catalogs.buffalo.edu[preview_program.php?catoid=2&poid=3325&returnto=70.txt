Management Science STEM MBA
UBs MBA programs are accredited by the prestigious AACSB International and nationally ranked by a variety of media for their quality and the return on investment we provide graduates. With an emphasis on experiential learning, our programs prepare graduates for leadership roles across a wide range of industries and functional areas.  UBs MBA programs have been preparing students for leadership positions in management since 1931. Our newest MBA option now includes a new full-time MBA in Management Science.  The Management Science MBA qualifies as a STEM (science, technology, engineering and mathematics) OPT degree program.  STEM OPT provides an additional 24 months Optional Practical Training (OPT) for F-1 visa holders who begin the program in fall 2021 or later. Like our other, non-STEM MBA programs, the new Management Science MBA featuring our long-standing and award-winning programs: LeaderCORE™ and Corporate Champions.
