Humanities Interdisciplinary: Film and Media Study MA
Film and media studies offers advanced research and professional training, for those interested in a multi-media/film related master’s level program. Students enjoy a wide selection of cross-disciplinary film and media courses, and work with scholars currently teaching in various departments and academic centers throughout the arts, humanities, and social sciences. Our curriculum is designed for graduates of film, media, gender, or cultural studies undergraduate programs, and for those with strong educational backgrounds in the liberal arts.
