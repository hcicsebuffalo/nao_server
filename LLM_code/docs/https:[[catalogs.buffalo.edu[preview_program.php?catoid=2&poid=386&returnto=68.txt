Chemical Engineering PhD
The Chemical Engineering PhD program at UB nurtures the next generation of leaders in the field. Candidates pursuing a PhD conduct independent research under the guidance of our faculty experts, developing deep expertise in their chosen area of specialization. Specializations include:
Biochemical and biomedical engineering
Nanoscale science and engineering
Computational science and engineering
