Engineering Management ME
Our nationally ranked online master’s in Engineering Management program provides graduate study for anyone who desires to enhance their technical competence to take leadership roles within an organization more effectively. Engineering Management ME graduates apply best practices and engineering approaches to make strategic decisions and shape personnel, finance, and operations policies.
