Childhood Education With Bilingual Extension (Grades 1-6) EdM
Early childhood and childhood education EdM programs are available for those who wish to pursue a bilingual specialization/extension. The programs are suitable for students who wish to obtain New York State certification to teach bilingual students birth through sixth grade, who already have initial teacher certification before entering the program, and who aspire to complete both a master’s degree in bilingual education and childhood education, or early childhood certification requirements.
