Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages EdM
The EDM in Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages (TESOL) program, is for you if you want to: teach in college-level English as a second language (ESL) programs; teach in adult-level ESL programs for immigrants and refugees in the United States; and teach in all levels internationally. The EdM TESOL degree has been considered the terminal professional degree for those seeking opportunities to teach ESL/ENL (English as a new language) in national and international institutions of higher education.
