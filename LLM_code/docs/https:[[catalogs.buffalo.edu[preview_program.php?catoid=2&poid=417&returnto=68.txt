Computational Science Advanced Certificate
The Advanced Certificate in Computational Science is a collaborative program offered by the Center for Computational Research (CCR) and several participating departments, namely Mathematics, Physics, Chemical Engineering, Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, and Computer Science and Engineering. This program seamlessly integrates the domains of mathematics and computer science to harness the potential of hardware and software in tackling complex computational challenges encountered in the fields of engineering and science.
