Data Sciences and Applications MPS
The Master of Professional Studies in Data Sciences and Applications program focuses on equipping students with the necessary skills in analytics to derive valuable insights from large and diverse datasets. Students will receive training in various data mining and machine learning techniques, enabling them to analyze and extract meaningful information from complex data effectively.
