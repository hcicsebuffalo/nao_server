Literacy Coaching Micro-Credential
Be a literacy hero! Be an elementary or secondary literacy coach! This micro-credential consists of four courses designed to support participants to develop and apply foundational knowledge of literacy, diversity, and equity to design, implement, and evaluate literacy curriculum and instruction; develop knowledge of literacy assessment and evaluation; and demonstrate the ability to support classroom teachers through professional learning, leadership, and coaching in meeting the literacy needs of students and designing learning environments.
For more information and to apply visit the Office of Micro-Credentials.
Course Requirements
Literacy Coaching Core (11 credits)
LAI 551LEC - Childhood Literacy Methods Credits: 1 - 3
LAI 552LEC - Middle Childhood/Adolescent Literacy Methods Credits: 1 - 3
LAI 557LEC - Clinical Literacy Assessment Credits: 3
LAI 562SEM - Theory and Research Elementary School Language Arts Credits: 3
Additional Requirements
Complete:
Provide a written summary of feedback to another teacher regarding their literacy teaching.
A reflection documenting the student’s understanding of the role of a literacy coach and their learning within the micro-credential program.
A reflection showcasing what they have learned and how they have met each of the 7 coaching standards. 
Total Credit Hours: 11
3.0 GPA required
  Instruction Method
Online (100 percent of the degree requirements can be fulfilled online) 
Average Time to Completion
3 semesters
