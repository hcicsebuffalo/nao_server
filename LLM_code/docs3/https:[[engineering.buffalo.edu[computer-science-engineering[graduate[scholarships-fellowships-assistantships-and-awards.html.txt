Scholarships, Fellowships, Assistantships, and Awards
Li Sun presents "Bringing Mobility-Awareness in WLANs using PHY Layer Information", at the CSE Grad Student Poster Session.  Li's PhD advisor is Dimitrios Koutsonikolas.  Photo credit: Ken Smith
Scholarships and assistantships are available to attract and support graduate students who have demonstrated exceptional academic performance and potential. 
These awards may include full or partial tuition scholarship and often include a stipend. They are designed to enable students to concentrate on coursework and/or research.
