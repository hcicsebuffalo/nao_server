Support Computer Science and Engineering
CSE student demonstrates technology to a young visitor 
Your gift to the Department of Computer Science and Engineering helps advance our mission to serve students, provide cutting-edge educational experiences, and advance world-class research. 
Donations have helped fund student scholarship and fellowships, chaired professorships, educational enhancement such as lecture series, as well as critical labs and research centers.
Your impact
Here are a few examples of how CSE alumni and friends have helped support our students and faculty:
The creation of a fund to advance our research and understanding of Blockchain technology.
Endowed fellowship to attract high-caliber graduate students to the department.
Donations support our undergraduate research and co-curricular activities like our annual Hack-a-thon event.
Named Chair in Software Engineering that is advancing our research abilities.
